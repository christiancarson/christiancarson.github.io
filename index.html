I am a rising conservation scientist living on the traditional and unceded territory of the Səlílwətaɬ (Tsleil-Waututh), Skwxwú7mesh (Squamish), xʷməθkwəyəm̓ (Musqueam), kwikwəƛ̓ əm (Kwikwetlem), q̓icə̓ y̓ (Katzie), Kwantlen and Semyome (Semiahmoo) Nations. I am currently a Master's of Resource Management (MRM) student at Simon Fraser University under the supervision of Dr. Douglas Braun (Aquatic Research Cooperative (ARC) Institute) and Dr. Jonathan Moore (Salmon Watersheds Lab), where I am investigating how stream temperatures respond to riparian headwater forest harvest in mid-to-large-sized catchments. My research interests are situated within the fields of conservation biology & ecology, where I aim to produce actionable science that is rooted in community goals and enhanced by technoscience.
